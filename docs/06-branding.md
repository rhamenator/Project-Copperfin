# Branding

## Chosen Working Codename

Copperfin Studio

This is a working project identity meant to avoid direct reuse of Microsoft/FoxPro/VFP naming.

## Naming Rationale

- sounds like an original platform, not a patch or clone
- avoids `Fox`, `VFP`, and `Visual FoxPro`
- feels durable and tool-oriented
- can extend into a product family cleanly

Product family suggestion:

- Copperfin Studio
- Copperfin Runtime
- Copperfin Reports
- Copperfin Migrator
- Copperfin Shield
- Copperfin Gateway

## Candidate Taglines

- Legacy Data. Modern Backbone.
- Keep The System. Upgrade The Platform.
- Business Apps, Rebuilt For Now.
- Clean-Room Compatibility For Real Work.

## Visual Direction

The logo avoids animals entirely.

The mark combines:

- a grid/data motif
- a shield/harbor shape for trust and modernization
- a copper-forward color palette

## Palette

- Copper: `#C66A2B`
- Deep Slate: `#1F2937`
- Sea Teal: `#2D7D7A`
- Sand: `#E7D9C8`
- White: `#F8FAFC`

## Typography Direction

- strong geometric sans for product identity
- practical monospace in docs/tooling
